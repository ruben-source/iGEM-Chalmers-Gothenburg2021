The Team

We are a group of 8 interdisciplinary students 
aiming at developing a programmable cell factory for tunable  fatty-acid production. Why fatty acids? Fatty acids are used in many industrial processes like food and medicine, but are currently extracted from eg. palm oil which causes deforestation.  
With our project and diverse knowledge we work together towards a more sustainable production of fatty acids, and thus towards a more sustainable world.
